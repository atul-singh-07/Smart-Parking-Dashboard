About SmartPark

SmartPark is an IoT-based parking management system that uses ESP32 and ultrasonic sensors to detect vehicle presence and update slot availability in real-time on the website.

Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion, save fuel, and digitize parking infrastructure with smart technology.
