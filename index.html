Smart Parking Management System is an IoT-based solution designed to solve real-world urban parking problems. Using ESP32, Ultrasonic Sensors, and Firebase Realtime Database, we provide live parking slot monitoring, EV charging management, pre-booking, and smart dashboard analytics.

About SmartPark

SmartPark is a full-stack IoT-based parking management system that uses ESP32 microcontrollers and ultrasonic sensors to detect vehicle presence and update slot availability in real-time on the web dashboard.

Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion, save fuel, digitize parking infrastructure, and integrate EV charging with smart technology for modern cities.
