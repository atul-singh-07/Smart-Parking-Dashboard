About Our Project

Smart Parking Management System is an IoT-based solution designed to solve real-world parking problems. Using ESP32, Ultrasonic Sensors, and Firebase Realtime Database, we provide live parking slot monitoring, pre-booking, and smart dashboard analytics for better urban parking management.
